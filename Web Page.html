Capabilities

What ArtMind Sees

Our multimodal AI combines computer vision, developmental psychology, and educational science into a single, powerful analysis engine.

🧠

Emotion Detection

Identifies 14 emotional states from drawing patterns, pressure, shape symbolism, and spatial composition using trained psychological models.

🎨

Color Psychology

Maps dominant and secondary colors against cross-cultural emotional associations and developmental color theory frameworks.

✏️

Creativity Scoring

Evaluates originality, detail richness, symbol usage, and compositional complexity against age-matched developmental benchmarks.

📋

Personalized Reports

Generates clear, parent-friendly and educator-ready reports with actionable recommendations and developmental context.

🔒

Privacy First

All images are processed locally and never stored. Designed with GDPR and child data protection regulations in full compliance.

📈

Longitudinal Tracking

Track emotional development across multiple drawings over time to observe growth, shifts in wellbeing, and creative evolution.

Process

How the AI Workflow Works

From a scanned drawing to a full psychological report in under 10 seconds.

01

Image Upload

The child's drawing is securely uploaded and preprocessed — normalized, denoised, and segmented into analyzable regions.

02

Vision AI Scan

Our CNN-based vision model detects objects, figures, colors, spatial relationships, and stroke patterns across the image.

03

Psychological Mapping

Detected features are cross-referenced against validated psychological frameworks from developmental and child psychology research.

04

Report Generation

A language model synthesizes all insights into a human-readable emotional profile with visual charts and recommendations.
